Process

How It Works

Simple, transparent workflow — from report to resolution

1

Citizen Reports

Submit a complaint with a photo, location and category. It lands in the common bin immediately.

2

Department Claims

The relevant department picks up the complaint and assigns a field worker to the job.

3

Worker Resolves

The assigned worker fixes the issue and uploads a proof photo to mark it resolved.

4

Citizen Verifies

The reporter confirms the fix. The admin closes the ticket and rewards the citizen.
